Short and Sweet

Hi! I'm currrently a games developer with a passion for self help, meditation and Linux.

What are my interests?

Learning, linux, drawing, music and games.

  • I've always been a creative drawer since I was a child, this has allowed me to have an appreciation for artwork, which includes any type of graphic.
  • During my teenage years I went through a music experience, like many teenagers, had one earphone in pretty much all the time, I am interested in a wide variety of music from Classical to Trance to Heavy Rock and everything inbetween. However recently have made the transition to actively listen to classical, orchestra and instrumental music, as the words we listen to in songs act as affirmations which can have an effect on your mind state.
  • Again, during my teenage years I was a huge gamer, I've played anything from 2D arcade games such as "Cube Runner" to fully intensive games such as "Ark Survival Envolved". However, when I entered around my mid 20's I realised that these games were taking up too much of my time, so I decided to make the big step to stop 'gaming', however a few years later, I came up with an idea, with my huge knowledge of games, why not use it to make other games, so that's what I'm doing in the background, when I finalise a game I'll display it on my portfolio.
  • Linux is a huge part of my life because I use it in my personal time. Quite recently in 2021 I have created my own Arch based ISO, with custom software allowing you to install the system through an open source piece of software. The idea behind creating my own version of Arch was to focus more on creating an operating system that can induce a positive mind frame, cutting out notifications that take too much of your attention, plus using subtle positive colours such as a light blue, light orange, light purple etc. for a theme.

How did I start this journey?

I've always been interested in coding from a young age however I've never known where to start, it wasn't until the Cryptocurrency hype that it rekindled the embers of my enthusiasm. I started to learn Solidity (Ethereum's smart contract language), but in order to use it effectively I needed to learn the basics, HTML, JS and CSS so I could display, collect and collate information for the smart contract and it was then that my journey started. During this time, I rediscovered my passion for art and music, it was when I started to incorporate this passion into coding and development which exponentially fanned the flames on my enthusiasm which is burning brightly.
